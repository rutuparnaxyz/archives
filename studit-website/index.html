About us

Study More In Less Time

MindQue India Pvt. Ltd, a technology-driven entrepreneurship project has designed a venture Studit, which centralizes on making a child’s educational career bright. We mainly focus on the safety and fundamentals of the studies and prepare them to fight, with the competitions. So to do that, we act as a one-stop solution to provide home tutors at the doorstep and we believe in creating a transparent interface between tutor-student-parent.

Tech-driven

We are enforcing technology to bring discipline to this vastly unorganized sector of delivering quality home tuitions by being constantly vigilant in providing good home tutors and mapping their performance keeping the focus on Curiosity, Learning & Internalization for the students both in offline and online mode.

Bridge

The organization takes pride that its innovation lies in providing the technology-driven a disciplined platform for both teachers and students where both can grow simultaneously. with time we are looking forward to making this platform a secured place for teachers so that they can opt home-tutoring as a full-time career option.

Achievement

Studit is the first home tutoring organization in India, who gives students, a study environment at there home. Who provides options for students to choose the best tutors for them. We are offering highly qualified and verified teachers for students from LKG to 12th Class.
